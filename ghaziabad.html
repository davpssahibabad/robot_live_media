Robot Details

This autonomous patrolling robot uses caterpillar tracks, a Raspberry Pi 3 Model B, motion and thermal sensors, and an HD camera to monitor streets in Ghaziabad. It can detect movement, unusual heat (possible fire or emergencies), trigger a siren on theft attempts, and notify emergency services through connected systems.

Key features:
• Continuous patrolling with tracked wheels.
• Motion (PIR) and thermal sensing for people and fire.
• Anti-theft detection with loud siren.
• Headlights for night patrol and live video stream to this dashboard.
